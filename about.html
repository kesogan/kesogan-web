Why Hire a Virtual Assistance

There are times when you need to hire someone to work in your office. But if you don't need physical tasks like changing ink in the printer or signing for product delivery, opt for a virtual assistant.

Often, finding the right person involves a tedious cycle of recruiting, hiring, training and firing that you repeat until you find the right person. Once you transfer these tedious tasks to me, the increase in your productivity and sales will have a significant impact on your business, your income and your customer base.

Save Time

The time you spend on administrative tasks every day is time you're not spending growing your business, making more sales, or connecting with more people. You could use this time for your personal priorities like hobbies or spending time with family and friends. Delegating the little things lets you choose the best use of your precious time.

No salary

As you will have understood, a virtual administrative assistant is not an employee. I am an independent worker. So you call on me as a supplier. You do not have to pay me a salary, you only pay me for the work done, upon presentation of an invoice. No more mandatory declarations, deadlines to meet, organizations to contact and steps to take.

You do not need any equipment or premises

First, I work remotely, from home. It is therefore not necessary for you to buy or rent premises or to invest in office equipment (chairs, desks, shelves, filing cabinets, printer, etc.) or even in supplies. As we know, administrative tasks require a minimum of organization and supplies (pens, notebook, highlighters, index cards, paper, etc.). In addition, a commercial lease being generally long, it is sometimes difficult for small businesses or self-employed people to commit to a long term from the start of their activity. Finally, the rent or the monthly payments that you will not spend on the occupation of a room can be invested in your activities as well as to entrust more tasks to your favorite personal assistant. Second, I already have software to work with that I use every day for other clients. So I have already invested in the necessary licenses to provide you with quality work.

More productivity and less overwork

When you run a small business, you are responsible for a variety of tasks. You may feel too tired. If you often end the day feeling like you've accomplished very little even though you're exhausted, it's likely that your productivity is being undermined by too many small tasks. Having someone else do the initial small tasks lets you focus on the big picture and takes the decision fatigue away, allowing you to be more productive and feel less stressed.

Expertise

You trust different experts in your privacy. So why not also do it in your daily professional life by entrusting your administrative tasks to a virtual assistant? Indeed, using a personal assistant will allow you to entrust me with tasks for which you do not have sufficient skills or knowledge. I could offer you a neat layout, faultless texts or even publications for your professional social networks and in the image of your company.

Time Period

One of the other many advantages of trusting a virtual assistant is that you choose what you want to delegate to me. This can be just for a particular project, or just to help you set up a good organization at the start of your business. So you don't have to hire me with the fear of having to fire me if things don't go the right way. Also, finding an employee for only a few hours a week is often complicated. In addition, you have the possibility to customize the amount you pay at any time according to your evolution. I adapt to your rhythm and your needs.

Network

A personal assistant often has several clients. Working with them "from the inside", I know the strengths of each. Are you looking for a specialist or an expert in a particular field? Do not hesitate to ask me for advice. I surely know, thanks to my network, the person or the company that will be perfect in your situation. And if not, I can do some research to find it.

Privacy

One of the main questions of an entrepreneur is often “And confidentiality in all this? " Do not panic ! A virtual assistant, like an employee, can very well sign a confidentiality clause and thus undertake not to disclose information about your business, your customers or your collaborators. This is quite possible and even a common practice. Thus, our professional relationship will be based on transparency from the start. I can even write the confidentiality clause for you.
